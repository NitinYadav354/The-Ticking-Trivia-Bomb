The Ticking Trivia Bomb

Welcome to "The Ticking Trivia Bomb"! Test your knowledge and see if you can defuse the bomb by answering trivia questions correctly before bomb explodes!

How to Play:

  1. There will be a series of trivia questions.
  2. You must answer each question correctly to move on to the next one.
  3. You have a limited amount of time to answer maximum questions before the bomb "explodes".
  4. For every correct answer, 5s added to your timer.
  5. For every incorrect answer, 10s subtracted from your timer.
